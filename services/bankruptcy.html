Bankruptcy

If you are considering filing for Bankruptcy, a clear and accurate financial picture is of the utmost importance. Whether its Chapter 7 or Chapter 13, the value of your real estate will be a major component of this picture. Only an appraisal report , prepared by an experienced and certified professional can help you establish this value. Our appraisal reports are accepted and used by New York Courts, the IRS , and other Agencies. All of our findings, conclusions and reports are confidential as required by the Uniform Standards of Professional Appraisal Practice (USPAP).
