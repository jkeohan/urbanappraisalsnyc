Divorce

A divorce proceeding will oftentimes involve the value of real estate either owned by one or both parties . This situation will call for a well supported, professional opinion of market value that is defensible in court. This opinion will be relied on by attorneys, accountants and other interested parties in divorces and other disputes requiring a value being placed on property. In cases, where the divorce proceedings require a retrospective appraisal with a unique effective date, we are very familiar with the procedures and requirements necessary to perform this type of assignment. All of our findings, conclusions and reports are confidential as required by the Uniform Standards of Professional Appraisal Practice (USPAP).
