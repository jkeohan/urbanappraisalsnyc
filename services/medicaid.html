Medicaid

Applying for medicaid can be a difficult and challenging process. Medicaid requires proof of assets. In the case of real estate, an appraisal report by a professional is required to substantiate the actual value of the real estate. Our team fully understands the requirements of this process, and our reports are accepted by medicaid.

Sponsoring a relative to become a permanent resident of the United States is a difficult process. The Unite States Citizenship and Immigration Services requires an Affidavit of Support that may require proof of assets. In the case of real estate, an appraisal by a professional is required to substantiate the value of the real estate in question. Our team meets the requirements and our reports are accepted by the Federal agencies involved.
