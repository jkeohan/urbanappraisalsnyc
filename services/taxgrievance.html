Tax Grievance

Quite often , the taxes which you pay for your property will be higher than expected for the area. In this instance, a grievance can be initiated to challenge the existing assessment of your property. The first step is to determine the current market value of your property. Information obtained from the Internet ( Zillow, Trulia, etc.,) will generally not be acceptable. This is a case where only a professionally prepared appraiser report will suffice. By establishing the current market value of your property, our team of NY State Certified Appraisers can assist you in your quest to reduce your property tax burden.
